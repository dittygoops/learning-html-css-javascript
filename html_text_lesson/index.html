Headers

How do they look?

<h1> </h1> - Header 1

<h2> </h2> - Header 2

<h3> </h3> - Header 3

<h4> </h4> - Header 4

<h5> </h5> - Header 5

<h6> </h6> - Header 6

As we go down the headers, they get smaller and smaller.


Why do we need headers?

Headers help us organize our information.

Additionally, headers tell engines and other developers what key words are key.


Paragraphs

How do they look?

<p> </p> - Paragraph


Why do we need paragraphs?

Paragraphs help us display our text with meaningful spacing.

Without paragraphs, we cannot properly space our text. Rather, everything would be in one long line.


Bold and Italics

How do they look?

<strong> </strong> - Bold

<em> </em> - Italics


Why do we need bold and italics?

Bold and italics help us emphasize certain parts of our text.

Without bold and italics, all our text would be simple and boring.

Additionally, bold and italics inform accessibility tools what parts of our text should be emphasized.
